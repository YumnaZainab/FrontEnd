Advance Level

Physical strikes using the leg, in unison usually with an area of the knee or lower using the foot, heel, tibia, ball of the foot, blade of the foot, toes or knee.

Punching techniques in karate are called tsuki or zuki. Contact is made with the first two knuckles (seiken).

Blocking is the act of stopping or deflecting an opponent's attack for the purpose of preventing injurious contact with the body.

Kata is a Japanese word describing detailed patterns of movements practiced either solo or in pairs.

Karate sparring, known as kumite, is a sort of exercise performed between at least two karate practitioners, using the techniques learned from the kihon and kata
